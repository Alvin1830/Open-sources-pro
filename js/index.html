Welcome to Culture Guide Page

Set in the Indian Ocean in South Asia, the tropical island nation of Kanady Srilanka has a history dating back to the birth of time. It is a place where the original soul of Buddhism still flourishes and where nature's beauty remains unspoilt.

This is an island of magical proportions, once known as Serendib , Taprobane , the pearl of the indian Ocean, and ceylon. Discover refreshingly Srilanka.
